Multi-Channel Water Electrolysis Test System

Product Overview

The multi-channel water electrolysis single-cell test equipment is a multi-channel parallel testing platform developed for alkaline water electrolysis, AEM water electrolysis, and seawater electrolysis hydrogen production fields. Its core advantage lies in supporting 2-8 channels of independent operation (users can specify the number of channels), enabling simultaneous atmospheric pressure testing of multiple single cells or short stacks, achieving parallel evaluation and verification of various materials such as powder catalysts, electrodes, and membrane electrodes, significantly shortening multi-sample testing cycles and remarkably improving efficient testing and screening capabilities. The equipment integrates electrolyte circulation system, nitrogen purging unit, pressure regulation unit, gas-liquid separation unit, gas analysis preprocessing unit, PLC acquisition and control unit, human-machine operation unit, and safety monitoring unit. Each channel can independently conduct polarization curves, electrochemical testing (requires optional electrochemical workstation), online monitoring of oxygen in hydrogen/hydrogen in oxygen concentration, sensitivity testing, durability testing, etc., and the operating parameters of each channel (such as flow rate, temperature, voltage, current) can be independently controlled without mutual interference.
