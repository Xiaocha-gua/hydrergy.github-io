Multi-Channel Water Electrolysis Hydrogen System

High-Power Output · Independent Control · High-Efficiency Testing

Product Overview

Advanced 8-channel parallel testing platform designed for research institutes and industrial applications. Enables simultaneous multi-group electrolysis experiments with significantly improved efficiency and data analysis capabilities.

Core Advantages

  • 8-Channel Parallel: Simultaneous operation with independent parameter control per channel
  • Multi-Application: Alkaline, PEM, AEM, and seawater electrolysis compatible
  • Independent Control: Dedicated power, gas processing, and circulation modules per channel
  • Modular Design: Flexible configuration and expansion with compact footprint
  • High Power Output: Up to 50kW per channel for lab-to-pilot scale testing
  • Safety First: Comprehensive protection systems ensure reliable operation
